Poem

    Nu ƿe herıgan sculon|      heofonrıces ƿeard

    metodes mıhte[1]|      ond hıs modgeþanc

    ƿeorc ƿuldorgodes|      sƿa he ƿundra fela

    éce[2] drıhten      ord| astealde

5     he ærest sceop      eorðan bear|num

    heofon to hrofe      halıg scyp|pend

    þe mıddangeard      manncynnes| ƿeard

    éce[3] drıhten      æfter teode|

    fyrum foldan      frea ælmıhtıg⸵[4]|

Notes

[1]mıhte|] <h> written over ink smudges.

[2]éce] With accent over first <e> in lighter ink.

[3]éce] With accent over first <e> in lighter ink.

[4]ælmıhtıg⸵|] Dobbie 1937 reads a point after this word.
